Places Must Visit

Epiquria

I have visited this place on 24th May 2021

A beautiful place to visit with family & friends on a beautiful evening! and,If you wanna see a movie a solution for that too!in front the epiquria mall there's a movie theatre so this gives you a movie+food +relaxation free! it's just a 3 in one package.On every 31st December night all the people come at the place and do a party and when its 12 all the people shout together! HAPPY NEW YEAR!! and this gives an icing to the cake.Overall my experience with the Epicuria is just amazing.

Gaur City Mall

I have visited this place on 13 January

Gaur City Mall is an amazing place.It has everything yiu need for fun including game center!It is a huge shopping complex built in Noida.It has over 40 stores! In total it has 3 floors The first floor is for shops the second floor is also for shops and the 3rd floor is a food court there are many reastaurants there so if you wanna have a light snack between shopping then you may reach the 3rd floor.on the first floor there are also some rides for children like the panda train and trampoline.Its a beautiful and enjoying place to visit!
